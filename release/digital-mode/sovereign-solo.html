Sovereign

You are trying to build a durable republic. Three shared tracks measure how it is doing:

  • Public Credit — the trust that holds the bond market together.
  • Public Resistance — the political strain in the western counties.
  • Industrial Capacity — the productive power of the new nation's workshops.

Each turn you roll, you land, and you choose. The game will guide you. The ledger will record everything.

A few things to know before you begin.

1
Your turn loop is Roll → Land → Choose → Consequence. The rail above the board will tell you what to do next.
2
Each round, every player takes one turn. The game does not end by a turn-counter — it ends by state. Pass Treasury Opens to complete a board circuit; build income and influence between crossings.
3
Three shared tracks — Public Credit, Public Resistance, Industrial Capacity — change as the Republic plays out.
4
Your portfolio appears in a strip beside the action rail. Use the "Panels" button on the right edge to see opponent details and the ledger.
5
Four times around ends it. The game ends when one of you has carried your faction around the Republic four times. Whoever holds the strongest ledger at that moment wins — not necessarily the one who got there first.
