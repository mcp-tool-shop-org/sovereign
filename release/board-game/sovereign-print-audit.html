§ 02Specific Fixes

P0 · must fix before first print

P0 · 01
Card-back coverage note

Cover currently says "print sheet 10 on reverse of card sheets" but nine backs covers only one card sheet. Edit the cover line to:

"Print sheet 10 once per card sheet you want backed — about seven copies for full coverage."

P1 · fix before second playtest

P1 · 02
Acts permanence cue

Rules don't tell you what to do with passed Acts (their +50 % effects are permanent). Add to § 09:

"Passed Acts are placed face-up beside the Track Board. Their effects remain in force until game end."
P1 · 03
Route landing rule explicit

§ 05 mentions route payments but doesn't say when you pay. Add one sentence:

"Routes pay when an opponent lands on the route; the payment scales with the number of routes the owner currently holds."
P1 · 04
Default vs Bankruptcy precedence

Add to § 15:

"If Default fires on a player with no upgrades, the cash penalty applies alone. If a player would be bankrupted by Default, resolve Default first, then bankruptcy."

P2 · clarifications

P2 · 05
Influence track-bonus split

§ 13 says "split 5 Influence among National Finance owners" — ambiguous with 2 – 4 owners. Rephrase to:

"Each player who owns at least one National Finance property gains ⌊ 5 / N ⌋ Influence, where N is the number of such players."
P2 · 06
Tax-space rules

§ 07 covers corners but not Federal Excise (space 4) or Speculation Scandal (space 38). Add § 07 a:

"Tax spaces: pay the amount shown to the bank. Speculation Scandal additionally moves Public Resistance +1."

P3 · nice-to-have

P3 · 07
Per-deck card backs in the master pack

The standalone Event-Cards file has distinct "Volatility" (red) and "Broadside" (navy) backs per deck. The master pack carries only the universal Treasury back. Two extra sheets would restore deck-identity-from-back.

P3 · 08
Property card icon repetition

All cards within a system share one icon — acceptable per the locked visual system, but the card name is the only discriminator. If players grab the wrong card during play, add tiny corner numerals (1 of 3 / 2 of 3 / 3 of 3) inside each system.

§ 04Ready-for-First-Print Verdict

Ready for first print

A group can print this pack today, cut it out, and play a rough first session.

The P0 fix is a one-sentence cover edit. The P1 fixes are clarifying rules sentences that do not change any number. None of the findings change a price, a track value, a corner rule, or an Act effect. Do the P0 and P1 edits before a second playtest and the document is camera-ready.

Recommended path: apply fixes 01 – 06, reprint sheets 1 and 28 – 29, run playtest 1 with the current pack, and gather feedback against the audit's open questions (Influence split, Default precedence).
