§ 03

Panels

Every information surface is a parchment panel with ink border, foil rule below the head, gilt surface-id badge at the right of each head. Inner card stroke is a half-pixel rule offset 4px from the border, which prints as a single hairline.

§ 07

§ 08

Cards

Market Shock and Republic Debate cards share a structure but have distinct chrome — Market Shock's band is split warning/ink, Debate's is solid ink. Both surface a dashed-rule effect block and three optional outcome chips (Credit / Resist / Indust).

§ 10

Failure-tier treatments

Three escalating tiers. Each fires a distinctly-styled ledger row AND a track-panel chip, so the two surfaces agree without text duplication. Crisis is non-terminal; Default and Rebellion both have reset semantics in the rules layer.
